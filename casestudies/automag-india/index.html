Automag India – Turning a Website into a B2B Lead Engine

How Impulse Digital helped Automag India move from zero visibility to a steady pipeline of high-quality B2B leads in under four months.

The Challenge

Zero Search Visibility

No keywords ranking in Google’s Top 10; Automag was invisible for its own offerings.

Minimal Lead Generation

Only 1–2 inbound B2B leads per month coming from the website.

Misaligned Traffic

Career page was the biggest traffic driver instead of revenue-driving product pages.

Poor Product Discovery

High-intent product queries like conveyor searches were not showing Automag anywhere.

Objective: Build strong organic visibility, attract qualified B2B buyers, and convert the website into a reliable lead-gen channel.

Our SEO Playbook

01

Keyword Research

Focused on high-intent B2B search terms mapped to Automag’s core conveyor and automation offerings.

02

On-Page Optimisation

Restructured key pages, refined copy, and aligned metadata to match buyer intent.

03

Technical SEO

Improved crawlability, internal linking, and site architecture for better discovery.

04

Content Optimisation

Refreshed content to answer real customer questions and push them closer to enquiry.

05

Blog & Support Content

Created supporting resources and interlinking to build topical authority.

06

Monitoring & Refinement

Continuous tracking, experimentation, and SEO tweaks to keep rankings climbing.

The Breakthrough

Between Dec 2024 and Mar 2025, Automag transitioned from practically invisible to consistently present for high-intent industry searches.

  • • Strong jumps across Top 50, Top 20, and Top 10 keyword buckets.
  • • High-intent “money” keywords started appearing on page one.
  • • Organic visibility became a predictable growth lever.

From zero to meaningful visibility in under 4 months.

Top 50 RankingsBefore: 0%  |  After: 75%
Top 20 RankingsBefore: 0%  |  After: 50%
Top 10 RankingsBefore: 0%  |  After: 25%

Traffic Growth

48% increase in organic traffic in just 90 days.

Product, home, and contact pages now drive the bulk of traffic — not the career section.

In short, traffic is now aligned with revenue goals instead of vanity metrics.

The Impact

Ranking Wins

Key Takeaway

Focused B2B SEO doesn’t just boost traffic—it transforms pipelines. In under four months, Automag India went from zero online visibility to being an industry reference in search results and lead generation.
